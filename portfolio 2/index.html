About Me

From the Yard to the Dashboard

I started on fabrication sites — reading drawings, tracking weld progress, and coordinating supervisors across complex piping and structural packages. That frontline experience taught me how projects actually move, stall, and recover.

Today I translate that field knowledge into structured data: Primavera P6 schedules, Power BI dashboards, and Python pipelines that turn raw progress logs into actionable insights. I build tools that connect what happens on the shop floor to what leadership sees in the boardroom.

My focus is fabrication and EPC project control — where schedule integrity, cost visibility, and predictive metrics matter most.
